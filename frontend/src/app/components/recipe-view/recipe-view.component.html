Traditional Valencian Paella

Spanish Gluten Free Traditional

Authentic Valencian recipe with traditional ingredients: chicken, rabbit, garrofó, green beans, bomba rice and saffron. An explosion of Mediterranean flavors.

Chef María González

Published 2 days ago

Time 30 min
Servings 6
Rating 4.8
Difficulty Medium

Total Time: 75 minutes

1

Prepare the sofrito

In the paella pan, heat olive oil and brown the chicken and rabbit. Add the grated tomato and cook until reduced.

2

Add the vegetables

Add the green beans and garrofó. Sauté for 5 minutes to integrate the flavors.
